# Credible Finance

- Category: remittance rail and FX infrastructure
- Type: accelerator company / builder project from Colosseum corpus
- Hackathon: Cypherpunk, September 2025
- Prize: `2nd Place - Stablecoins`
- Accelerator: `Credible`, C4

## What they build

- USD-INR remittance rail for banks, fintechs, and businesses powered by stablecoins.

## Core customer problem

- high remittance fees
- slow cross-border payments
- poor FX pricing
- expensive USD-INR transfers

## Proposed solution

- API-first remittance rail
- guaranteed FX pricing
- stablecoin-backed cross-border movement
- liquidity aggregation

## Why it matters

- Strong evidence that cross-border B2B remittance remains one of the clearest stablecoin infrastructure wedges in the corpus.
