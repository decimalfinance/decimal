# StablPay

- Category: cross-border payment infrastructure
- Type: builder project from Colosseum corpus
- Hackathon: Radar, September 2024

## What they build

- Cross-border payment infrastructure converting local currencies to stablecoins for seamless global settlement.

## Core customer problem

- high cross-border fees
- slow international transfers
- currency volatility
- complex remittance operations

## Proposed solution

- fiat-to-stablecoin conversion
- automated settlement
- cross-border infrastructure

## Why it matters

- Earlier signal than some 2025 projects that cross-border stablecoin settlement has been a persistent builder theme across cohorts.
