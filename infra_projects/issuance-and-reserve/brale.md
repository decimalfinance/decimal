# Brale

- Category: stablecoin issuance API, reserve management, orchestration
- Stage: venture-backed; raised `$30M` in 2025
- Main buyer: businesses, fintechs, protocols, app chains, treasury operators

## What they build

- Stablecoin issuance and orchestration tooling with reserve and account abstractions.
- The product is shaped around letting businesses launch stablecoin products and operate them with a programmable control plane.

## Core customer problem

- Issuing and managing production stablecoins is operationally hard.
- Teams need KYB, accounts, addresses, banking interfaces, issuance mechanics, and compliance guardrails without rebuilding everything.

## Why this matters

- Brale is evidence that stablecoin issuance is no longer a protocol-only activity.
- The buyer is increasingly a business operator, not just a token engineer.

## Business shape

- API-first infrastructure.
- Likely monetization through issuance, orchestration, account-level services, and treasury economics.

## Research signals

- Useful reference for:
  - stablecoin-as-a-service
  - reserve and issuance middleware
  - compliance-heavy backend infra
