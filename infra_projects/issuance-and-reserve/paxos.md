# Paxos

- Category: issuer and regulated stablecoin infrastructure
- Stage: private company; public materials say it has raised over `$540M`
- Main buyer: regulated institutions, networks, wallets, merchants, enterprise partners

## What they build

- Stablecoin issuance and regulated digital-dollar infrastructure, including PYUSD partnership rails and the Global Dollar Network / USDG related infrastructure.

## Core customer problem

- Institutions want stablecoins with stronger compliance, reserve, and regulatory positioning.
- They need a provider that can bridge crypto-native rails with regulated financial expectations.

## Why this matters

- Paxos is a proof point that “compliance-heavy stablecoin infra” is not a niche.
- It also shows that big distribution often comes through partnership rather than direct consumer ownership.

## Business shape

- Regulated backend infrastructure with network and partnership distribution.

## Research signals

- Useful reference for:
  - regulated issuance
  - partner-led distribution
  - compliant stablecoin backend infrastructure
