# Equator

- Category: stablecoin issuance platform
- Type: builder project from Colosseum corpus
- Hackathon: Cypherpunk, September 2025

## What they build

- Stablecoin launch platform for institutions to issue compliant digital currencies with real-time proof-of-reserves.

## Core customer problem

- lack of compliant stablecoin infrastructure
- opaque reserve backing
- complex issuance for institutions
- regulatory compliance hurdles

## Proposed solution

- stablecoin factory architecture
- programmable mint / burn permissions
- on-chain proof-of-reserves
- privacy-oriented attestations

## Why it matters

- Important signal that issuance tooling itself remains an opportunity area, especially for institutional buyers.
