# Hylo

- Category: stablecoin protocol and reserve design
- Type: accelerator company / builder project from Colosseum corpus
- Hackathon: Radar, September 2024
- Prize: `2nd Place - Payments`
- Accelerator: `Hylo`, C2

## What they build

- Dual-token stablecoin protocol backed by Solana liquid staking tokens.

## Core customer problem

- stablecoin volatility
- centralized collateral risks
- underutilized staking liquidity
- capital inefficiency

## Proposed solution

- LST-backed stablecoin
- decentralized reserve structure
- productive collateral model

## Why it matters

- Shows that “stablecoin infrastructure” also includes reserve architecture and stablecoin design, not just payments and treasury tooling.
