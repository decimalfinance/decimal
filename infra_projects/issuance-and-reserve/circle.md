# Circle

- Category: issuance, settlement, developer infrastructure
- Stage: public company
- Main buyer: enterprises, developers, exchanges, financial institutions, wallets

## What they build

- Native USDC and related developer infrastructure including minting, treasury operations, and cross-chain movement.
- Circle is the canonical stablecoin infrastructure reference point for many builders.

## Core customer problem

- Teams need trusted dollar liquidity and a well-supported enterprise stack around it.
- They need settlement, issuance, and treasury movement with regulatory legitimacy and broad distribution.

## Why this matters

- Circle defines the baseline expectations for enterprise-grade stablecoin infra.
- If a new product depends on stablecoin liquidity, treasury movement, or enterprise APIs, Circle is usually part of the environment whether directly or indirectly.

## Business shape

- Large-scale issuer and infrastructure provider.
- Value comes from distribution, trust, liquidity, and enterprise integrations.

## Research signals

- Useful reference for:
  - how much of the market is issuance-led
  - developer expectations around stablecoin APIs
  - where a smaller startup should not compete directly
