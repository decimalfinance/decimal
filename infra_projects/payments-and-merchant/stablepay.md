# StablePay

- Category: merchant stablecoin payment platform
- Type: builder project from Colosseum corpus
- Hackathon: Breakout, April 2025

## What they build

- Enterprise payment platform for multi-stablecoin acceptance with auto-settlement and yield generation.

## Core customer problem

- stablecoin fragmentation
- merchant settlement complexity
- idle capital inefficiency
- payment security risks

## Proposed solution

- accept multiple stablecoins
- settle in preferred currency
- built-in yield on balances
- unified merchant payment gateway

## Why it matters

- Useful reference point for merchant-facing stablecoin acceptance combined with treasury economics.
