# Borderless Wallets

- Category: stablecoin payment orchestration
- Type: accelerator company / builder project from Colosseum corpus
- Hackathon: Cypherpunk, September 2025
- Accelerator: `LocalPay`, C3

## What they build

- Stablecoin QR code payment orchestration intended to replace traditional card rails.

## Core customer problem

- high merchant fees
- payment network friction
- slow settlement

## Proposed solution

- orchestration layer for direct merchant payments
- QR-based stablecoin checkout
- merchant-facing payment abstraction

## Why it matters

- Strong signal that payment orchestration is recurring enough to produce multiple adjacent teams, not just one-off wallet ideas.
