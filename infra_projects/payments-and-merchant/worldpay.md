# Worldpay

- Category: merchant settlement and payouts
- Stage: large incumbent payments company
- Main buyer: merchants, marketplaces, payout-heavy businesses

## What they build

- Stablecoin settlement and payout capabilities for enterprise payment flows.
- The important signal is that they let businesses use stablecoin rails without forcing them to become crypto-native operators.

## Core customer problem

- Merchants and global businesses want cheaper or more flexible payouts and settlement, but they do not want raw crypto operations.

## Why this matters

- Worldpay is direct evidence that stablecoin infrastructure demand exists inside mainstream payment operations.
- It also suggests that the biggest opportunity may be behind existing businesses rather than in new consumer wallets.

## Business shape

- Enterprise payment infrastructure.
- Value tied to payout and settlement flows, not speculative usage.

## Research signals

- Useful reference for:
  - merchant settlement
  - payout infrastructure
  - stablecoin plumbing for non-crypto-native businesses
