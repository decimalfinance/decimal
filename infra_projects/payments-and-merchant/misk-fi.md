# MISK.FI

- Category: stablecoin checkout and B2B settlement infrastructure
- Type: builder project from Colosseum corpus
- Hackathon: Cypherpunk, September 2025
- Tracks: DeFi, Infrastructure, Stablecoins

## What they build

- Stablecoin infrastructure for checkouts, subscriptions, B2B netting, and verifiable on-chain receipts.

## Core customer problem

- high payment processing fees
- complex B2B settlement
- lack of verifiable receipts
- inefficient subscription management

## Proposed solution

- hosted checkout
- mandate-based subscriptions
- multilateral B2B netting
- escrow services
- on-chain invoicing and receipts

## Primitives and stack

- Primitives: `stablecoin`, `escrow`, `subscription`, `payments`
- Tech stack: `solana`

## Target users

- merchants
- B2B companies
- SaaS providers
- ecommerce platforms

## Why it matters

- This is one of the clearest builder-project signals for a practical stablecoin business workflow rather than a speculative crypto app.
- It points to a need for stablecoin-backed finance operations, not just transfers.
