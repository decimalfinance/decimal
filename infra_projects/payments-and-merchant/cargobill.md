# CargoBill

- Category: supply-chain stablecoin payments
- Type: accelerator company / builder project from Colosseum corpus
- Hackathon: Breakout, April 2025
- Prize: `1st Place - Stablecoins`
- Accelerator: `CargoBill`, C3

## What they build

- Stablecoin payments platform for supply-chain logistics using multisig wallets and enterprise onramps.

## Core customer problem

- high logistics transaction costs
- slow cross-border payments
- liquidity constraints in supply chains
- operational friction for trade settlement

## Proposed solution

- multisig business wallets
- stablecoin payments for logistics operators
- fiat onramps
- cashback / liquidity features for operational spend

## Why it matters

- One of the strongest signals in the corpus that verticalized stablecoin infra may beat generic infra.
