# M0

- Category: shared stablecoin infrastructure, issuance and interoperability layer
- Stage: heavily funded infrastructure company; total funding reported at `$100M`
- Main buyer: issuers, wallets, apps, protocols, ecosystems

## What they build

- A shared stablecoin infrastructure layer that helps other products issue or distribute programmable dollars.
- M0 is closer to “network infrastructure for stablecoins” than a single consumer-facing app.

## Core customer problem

- Stablecoin issuers and apps do not want to rebuild shared liquidity, interoperability, and issuance rails from scratch.
- They need modular, programmable infrastructure that sits below the app layer.

## Why this matters

- M0 is evidence that there is a real market for “meta-stablecoin infrastructure.”
- This is one of the clearest references for a picks-and-shovels strategy rather than a branded-end-user-money-app strategy.

## Business shape

- Deep infra, platform-facing, ecosystem-facing.
- More likely to sell into sophisticated counterparties than consumers.

## Research signals

- Useful reference for:
  - shared liquidity systems
  - stablecoin network effects
  - infrastructure below the issuer/app layer
