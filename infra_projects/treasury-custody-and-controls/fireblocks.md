# Fireblocks

- Category: custody, treasury operations, institutional transaction control
- Stage: established institutional infrastructure company
- Main buyer: institutions, treasuries, fintechs, large operators

## What they build

- Institutional-grade wallet, policy, and treasury infrastructure.
- In the Solana context, Fireblocks is part of the custody / control / operational security layer around moving stablecoins safely at scale.

## Core customer problem

- Large firms need operational control, approvals, policy, custody, and auditable treasury workflows.
- This is not a retail wallet problem. It is a risk-control problem.

## Why this matters

- Fireblocks is a strong signal that stablecoin infra is not just about payments or liquidity.
- A large portion of the market is really treasury operating systems and movement controls.

## Business shape

- Enterprise software plus custody / policy workflow layer.
- High-trust B2B buyer.

## Research signals

- Useful reference for:
  - treasury OS ideas
  - workflow approvals
  - operator security
  - institutional movement tooling
