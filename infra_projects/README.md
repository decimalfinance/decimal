# Stablecoin Infra Project Corpus

This folder is a neutral research corpus of stablecoin infrastructure companies and builder projects relevant to Solana and adjacent stablecoin operations.

The goal is not to validate one product idea. The goal is to understand:

- what categories already exist
- who is building in each category
- which customer problems keep repeating
- what parts of the stack look crowded
- what parts of the stack still look underbuilt
