# LocalPay

- Category: stablecoin payment wallet / local payment rails
- Type: accelerator company / builder project from Colosseum corpus
- Hackathon: Breakout, April 2025
- Prize: `3rd Place - Stablecoins`
- Accelerator: `LocalPay`, C3

## What they build

- Non-custodial mobile wallet for QR-based stablecoin payments in emerging markets.

## Core customer problem

- low day-to-day utility of stablecoins
- lack of banking access
- high transaction costs
- hard merchant acceptance

## Proposed solution

- QR payments
- non-custodial wallet
- local payment rail integration
- stablecoin neobank-like UX

## Why it matters

- Strong signal that merchant and local-payment acceptance is one of the most repeated consumer/business stablecoin patterns in the corpus.
