# KAST

- Category: stablecoin neobank / money app
- Stage: heavily funded growth company; public materials cite `$10M` seed and `$80M` Series A
- Main buyer: consumers and businesses moving dollars globally

## What they build

- A stablecoin-powered neobank / money app experience.
- KAST sits higher in the stack than pure infra, but it is relevant because it packages stablecoin rails into something closer to a usable banking product.

## Core customer problem

- End users and operators want a familiar money experience backed by stablecoin settlement and global movement.

## Why this matters

- KAST is useful because it shows where stablecoin infra becomes an application.
- It helps separate infra opportunities from money-app opportunities.

## Business shape

- Consumer / fintech layer with heavy dependence on underlying stablecoin rails.

## Research signals

- Useful reference for:
  - where stablecoin infra ends and product begins
  - neobank-like wrappers on stablecoin rails
  - UX expectations once infra becomes productized
