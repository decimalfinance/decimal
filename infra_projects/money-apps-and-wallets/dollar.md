# DOLLAR

- Category: stablecoin wallet / payments / remittance
- Type: accelerator company / builder project from Colosseum corpus
- Hackathon: Breakout, April 2025
- Prize: `Honorable Mention - Stablecoins`
- Accelerator: `Credible`, C4

## What they build

- Stablecoin-powered wallet for instant cross-border USD transfers and payments with a Web2-style UX.

## Core customer problem

- currency inflation
- high remittance fees
- complex crypto onboarding
- lack of dollar access

## Proposed solution

- stablecoin wallet
- p2p payments
- QR-based spend
- fiat-to-stablecoin rails

## Why it matters

- Not pure infra, but a strong indicator of how stablecoin rails are being packaged into end-user money apps.
