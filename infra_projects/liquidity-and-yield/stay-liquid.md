# Stay Liquid

- Category: white-label yield infrastructure
- Type: builder project from Colosseum corpus
- Hackathon: Cypherpunk, September 2025

## What they build

- White-label stablecoin DeFi yield infrastructure for fintechs that want risk-managed returns while maintaining custody.

## Core customer problem

- compliance barriers to DeFi
- custodial risk
- yield fragmentation
- complex risk management

## Proposed solution

- white-label yield protocols
- non-custodial asset management
- diversification and risk engine
- stablecoin liquidity pools

## Why it matters

- Good signal that a meaningful subcategory exists around “yield infra for fintechs,” not just yield products for end users.
