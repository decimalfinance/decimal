# Perena

- Category: stablecoin liquidity layer, stable swap infrastructure
- Stage: early but serious; backed by strategic trading and market structure names
- Main buyer: DeFi apps, stablecoin projects, liquidity operators

## What they build

- A unified liquidity layer for stablecoins on Solana.
- The positioning is about reducing fragmentation and making stablecoin liquidity behave more like a usable infrastructure primitive.

## Core customer problem

- Stablecoin liquidity is fragmented across issuers, venues, and pools.
- Fragmentation makes routing, conversion, and stablecoin utility worse.

## Why this matters

- Perena is one of the strongest signals that liquidity fragmentation is itself a business opportunity.
- Not every stablecoin company needs to issue or hold dollars. Some can own the liquidity coordination layer instead.

## Business shape

- DeFi-native infrastructure with protocol / market-structure buyers.
- Likely monetization through liquidity concentration, routing relevance, and protocol position.

## Research signals

- Useful reference for:
  - unified stablecoin liquidity
  - swap layer infrastructure
  - “shared pool” or “settlement fabric” ideas
