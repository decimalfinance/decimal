# Reflect

- Category: yield-bearing stablecoins, stablecoin-as-a-service
- Stage: seed-stage company; public job materials cite `$3.75M` seed
- Main buyer: users, institutions, apps, developers

## What they build

- Yield-bearing dollar products and infrastructure around productive stablecoins.
- The pitch is not just “payments dollar.” It is “working capital dollar.”

## Core customer problem

- Idle stablecoin balances are economically inefficient.
- Operators want stablecoins that remain usable but can also earn or route yield.

## Why this matters

- Reflect is a strong signal that the market wants productive dollars, not just transport dollars.
- Any treasury-focused idea should consider yield behavior as part of the operating model.

## Business shape

- Product + infrastructure hybrid.
- Attractive where treasury balances, idle cash, or embedded yield matter.

## Research signals

- Useful reference for:
  - stablecoin yield products
  - treasury revenue on balances
  - stablecoin-as-a-service with extra economics
