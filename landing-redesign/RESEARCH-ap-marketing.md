# AP Marketing Research — synthesis for the landing redesign

Date: 2026-07-14. Three research rounds (traditional AP leaders; AI-forward AP-native
players; crypto/stablecoin B2B). Purpose: decide the hero, the section order, the
AI-vs-control framing, and how loudly to say "USDC/Solana" on the new landing page.

---

## The three questions we set out to answer

1. **How bold can the agent be as the story?** → Bolder than the whole traditional field, but with a discipline the AI-forward players prove out.
2. **How do we frame AI-does-the-work vs. you-stay-in-control?** → Pair them in the same breath, every time. Never let an autonomy claim stand alone.
3. **How loudly do we say USDC/Solana?** → Quietly. Lead with the business outcome; the rail is plumbing.

---

## Round 1 — Traditional AP leaders (Ramp, BILL, Tipalti, Brex, AvidXchange)

- **Time saved is the universal hero.** Ramp: "A month of AP done in minutes… 2.4x faster." Tipalti: "Get Hours Back Every Week." BILL: "8 hours/week saved." Every hero references time or speed.
- **AI does the mechanical steps, never the judgment steps.** Not one leader says the AI "approves" or "decides to pay." AI captures, codes, matches, routes; a human approves/pays. Ramp (most agent-forward) has a whole H2: "Customization that keeps you in control."
- **The AP loop has a fixed 5-verb vocabulary:** capture → code/match → approve/route → pay → reconcile/sync. Tipalti and Ramp name it almost like a taxonomy.
- **Trust proof = customer count + logos + one stat**, universally (70K+, 500K+, 8,000+).
- **Emotional register is relief/efficiency, not fear.** Fraud/compliance is always a mid-page pillar, never the hook.

**The opening this leaves us:** the leaders can't make autonomy safe, so they all stop at "route to a human." Our code-enforced gate lets the agent cross into paying safely. That seam — the agent that actually pays, because the guardrail is code — is exactly what the category avoids.

## Round 2 — AI-forward AP-native (Stampli, Vic.ai, Airbase→Paylocity, Melio, Mercury)

The crux for "hand the AI the work, not the keys." Stampli and Vic.ai reach the same mechanism from opposite directions:

- **Stampli reframes the verb.** Avoids "automates," uses **"operates"**: "Stampli AI operates the day-to-day work… so finance can stay focused on control." "Stampli AI always operates under human oversight. It does the work and surfaces it for the right person to review." "Your team stays in charge of approvals, strategy, and oversight." Formula: *AI = operator of tasks; human = owner of decisions.* Control is a category boundary (tasks vs. judgment), never a percentage.
- **Vic.ai earns autonomy with numbers.** Willing to say "without human review" and "straight into the ERP ready for payment" — but gated: "Once the AI is extremely confident across all predictions…" plus "97–99% accuracy, surpassing human-level," "85% no-touch rate by month 6." The human becomes "reviewers instead of processors," keeps standing override. Formula: *autonomy earned per-invoice via confidence; human = exception-handler with override.*
- **The shared discipline (copy rule for us):** neither claims 100% autonomy as a virtue. Both name what stays human. Both use **"review"** as the human's residual verb. Neither uses "autonomous" without qualifying it in the very next line. **Lead with the AI doing the labor, pair it with an explicit control clause in the same breath.**
- **Melio/Mercury sidestep the tension** by never leading with autonomy — AI is a mid-page convenience feature. Melio hero = payment flexibility; Mercury closing thesis = "Precision. Control. Speed." We are structurally the Stampli/Vic.ai posture (agent doing real work), not the Melio/Mercury one.
- **"Keys" out-concretes all of them.** Stampli says "oversight," Vic.ai says "override," Mercury says "approve." Our "not the keys" is a stronger, more literal metaphor — and it's literally true (self-custody + code-enforced gate).

## Round 3 — Crypto/stablecoin B2B (Request Finance, Sphere, Rain, Mural Pay, Franklin, Bitwave, Conduit, BVNK)

- **Nobody names "USDC" in a hero. Nobody uses "crypto" in a hero.** "Stablecoin" is the most technical word ever promoted to hero level, and mainstream-targeted players push even that to the subhead.
- **Mainstream-facing players hide the rail.** Mural Pay hero: "Global Accounts. Realtime Payments. One API." (zero crypto words); founder's line is "Don't Call It Crypto." Rain states it as a design principle: **"Modern payments under the hood. Familiar experiences on the surface."**
- **Legitimacy comes from borrowed trust, not crypto-reassurance:** bank/card-network names, SOC 2 / audit badges, named regulatory registrations — and flipping the script so the *legacy* rail is the risky one. Request Finance H2: "The alternative to the banks who failed your business." Sphere H2s are accusations at the status quo ("Cash tied up in pre-funding?", "Losing margin to FX swings?").
- **"No custody risk" reframed as a selling point** (Bitwave: "vendor retains full control of your funds"). This is *our* strongest available safety story — self-custody is a better line than "we're SOC 2," and it's true.

## Strategic conclusions for Decimal's landing

1. **Hero = the agent that pays, paired with the code-enforced control clause in one breath.** This is the one thing no competitor can say. Lead with autonomy (bolder than the field), immediately bound it with "not the keys" (the discipline the AI-forward players prove is necessary). Keep "review" as the human's verb.
2. **Sell time/relief like everyone else — but our time claim is bigger** because the agent crosses into payment, not just prep. "The month-end that runs itself," not "AP in minutes."
3. **Demote the rail.** Move "Built on Solana / Secured by Squads / Settles in USDC" OUT of the hero/trust-strip lead. Say "global payments in a vendor's own currency" up top; USDC/Solana becomes an implementation detail lower down. Our current trust strip is more crypto-forward than any mainstream competitor's hero.
4. **Make self-custody the safety hero, not a feature cell.** "Your money never leaves your account" is borrowed-trust-grade legitimacy that is also literally true and un-fakeable by competitors who hold float.
5. **Flip the script on the legacy rail** (Request Finance / Sphere pattern): the 3% FX, the multi-day wire, the correspondent banks are the broken thing; we're the boring fix.
6. **The GL-coding engine and the policy/guardrail layer deserve real sections**, not grid cells — they're what we've actually built since the current page was written, and they're the substance behind "safe autonomy."
7. **Trust proof:** we lack customer counts/logos (pre-launch). Substitute the concrete, provable claims: self-custody, code-enforced limits, on-chain receipt, QuickBooks two-way. Concrete-and-true beats logos-we-don't-have.
